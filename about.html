About

I lead applied AI programmes that require more than strong models. My work connects strategy, product choices, technical architecture, delivery discipline, stakeholder adoption, and alignment to business value.

What I do

I work best where AI, machine learning, and data science need to move beyond experimentation into real-world use: regulated healthcare, enterprise decision-support, operational analytics, NLP, and AI products that must earn trust from both technical and non-technical stakeholders. I help organisations turn AI ambition into deployed systems, whether the challenge is machine learning, data science, decision-support, NLP, or enterprise AI capability.

What distinguishes me

  • Strategy to deployment: I help shape the path from opportunity and prioritisation through to practical delivery.
  • Technical depth with delivery focus: I combine hands-on understanding of ML, NLP, data engineering, interoperability, and GenAI with practical decisions about reliability, integration, and adoption. Strong data and interoperability foundations are often what determine whether AI can move beyond a promising prototype into something deployable.
  • Leadership in complex environments: I align clinicians, domain experts, product owners, engineers, analysts, and senior stakeholders around outcomes.

What I'm known for in practice

  • Leading multi-workstream applied AI delivery in a regulated paediatric healthcare environment
  • Building a genomics NLP pathway from PDF reports to FHIR mapping and clinician-facing dashboards
  • Co-leading interpretable clinical ML work for CKD progression modelling
  • Delivering enterprise RAG / LLM systems with governance and traceability requirements

While healthcare is the clearest recent proof point, my broader experience spans telecommunications, financial services, retail, accounting, and the UK public sector.

Based in Cyprus, open to remote, hybrid, and international engagements.
