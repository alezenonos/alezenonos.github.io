About

I am an AI leader, researcher, and hands-on builder of production AI systems. I lead AI, machine learning, and data initiatives across the full lifecycle, from problem framing and model development through to delivery, deployment, and stakeholder engagement. My experience spans enterprise, public sector, consulting, and regulated environments, including high-impact work across healthcare, telecoms, financial services, insurance, property analytics, and AI-enabled data products.

I studied at UCL and Imperial College London and completed a PhD in Artificial Intelligence. Over the course of my career, I have built experience across organisations including Toshiba Research Europe, Capgemini, Accenture, KPMG, and Roche, giving me a combination of technical depth, commercial delivery experience, and credibility in complex environments.

What I bring

Technical depth with delivery focus

Hands-on understanding of NLP, ML, interoperability, GenAI, and decision-support systems combined with practical decisions about reliability, integration, and adoption.

Leadership across disciplines

Alignment of clinicians, domain experts, engineers, product leads, analysts, and senior stakeholders around shared outcomes.

High-stakes operating context

Experience in settings where regulation, safety, governance, legacy systems, and organisational complexity are central—not edge cases.

My delivery experience is cross-sector by design, spanning healthcare, telecoms, finance, insurance, and property analytics, as well as broader enterprise AI, operational analytics, and research-led problem solving.

Cyprus is a European Union member state. I hold full EU rights and am open to roles across the EU and the UK. Remote and hybrid arrangements preferred.
