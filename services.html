How I work

Four capability areas I lead in senior AI roles and advisory engagements. Each one reflects how I actually deliver, not generic service lines.

Applied AI strategy and delivery

Framing the right problems, shaping the delivery approach, and moving AI work from experimentation into deployed capability. This includes prioritisation, technical direction, and maintaining delivery pace across cross-functional teams.

Clinical and regulated AI

Leading AI programmes in environments where trust, interpretability, interoperability, and governance are central to whether the work succeeds — from clinician-facing tooling to FHIR-based interoperability and safe LLM use.

AI products, decision-support tools, and LLM systems

Designing and delivering AI products, including predictive ML, LLM, and retrieval-augmented systems, built for real workflows, operational controls, and adoption rather than demos.

Cross-functional leadership in complex environments

Aligning clinicians, engineers, data scientists, executives, and delivery teams around shared outcomes. Credible with technical teams and with senior stakeholders, from C-level to domain experts.

Best-fit engagements

Strongest where an organisation needs one senior leader who can connect AI strategy, technical judgement, stakeholder leadership, and delivery pace — especially in regulated, commercially important, or operationally complex environments.

FAQ

What kinds of AI roles do you take on?

Senior AI leadership roles such as Head of AI, Director of AI / Applied AI, Principal AI, and Clinical AI Lead, as well as selective advisory work on complex AI programmes.

Do you only work in healthcare?

No. My delivery experience spans healthcare, telecoms, financial services, insurance, and property analytics, as well as broader enterprise AI and decision-support problems.

Do you still go hands-on technically?

Yes. My value is strongest at the intersection of leadership, technical judgement, and practical delivery. I remain hands-on with NLP, predictive ML, LLMs, RAG, and interoperability work where it matters.

Are you open to advisory engagements?

Yes. I am open to strategic advisory discussions, specialist delivery support, and senior roles where applied AI needs to move from concept into trusted, deployable systems.

Where are you based?

I am based in Cyprus, an EU member state, and open to remote, hybrid, and international engagements across the EU and UK.
